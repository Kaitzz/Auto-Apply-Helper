# Privacy Policy for Job Application Autofill

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Overview

Job Application Autofill ("the Extension") is a browser extension that helps users automatically fill job application forms. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
The Extension stores the following information that you provide:
- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Professional information (LinkedIn URL, GitHub URL, website)
- Education information (school, degree, discipline, years)
- Work authorization status
- EEO demographic information (optional)
- Resume files (PDF, DOC, DOCX)
- Cover letter files (PDF, DOC, DOCX)

### Where Data Is Stored
**All data is stored locally on your device** using Chrome's built-in storage API (`chrome.storage.local`). 

- ✅ Data stays on YOUR computer
- ✅ Data is NOT sent to any external servers
- ✅ Data is NOT shared with third parties
- ✅ Data is NOT used for analytics or tracking

## Data Usage

Your data is used solely for:
- Auto-filling job application forms when you click "Fill Application"
- Uploading your resume/cover letter to job application forms

## Data Sharing

**We do not share your data with anyone.** The Extension:
- Does NOT transmit data to external servers
- Does NOT use analytics services
- Does NOT include advertising
- Does NOT sell or share your information

## Data Security

Your data is protected by:
- Chrome's built-in storage encryption
- Data remains local to your browser profile
- No network transmission of personal data

## Data Retention

Your data remains stored until you:
- Clear the Extension's data
- Uninstall the Extension
- Clear your browser data

## Your Rights

You can:
- View all stored data in the Extension's side panel
- Modify your data at any time
- Delete your data by clearing Extension storage or uninstalling

## Permissions Explained

The Extension requests these permissions:

| Permission | Why It's Needed |
|------------|-----------------|
| `storage` | Save your profile data locally |
| `activeTab` | Access the current job application page to fill forms |
| `sidePanel` | Display the Extension's user interface |
| `tabs` | Detect when you're on a job application site |

## Third-Party Services

The Extension does NOT use any third-party services, APIs, or analytics.

## Children's Privacy

This Extension is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
